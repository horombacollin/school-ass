Site Name

The site name for this Chamber of Commerce is "Soweto Chamber of Commerce". The name was selected to reflect the locality of the chamber and to emphasize its role in promoting the business community and economic growth in the township of Soweto.

Site Purpose

  • To provide a comprehensive directory of local businesses in Soweto, allowing residents and visitors to easily find the products and services they need.
  • To serve as a platform for promoting local events, workshops, and networking opportunities, fostering collaboration and community engagement within the business ecosystem.

Scenarios

  • A local resident wants to find a trusted restaurant in Soweto for a special occasion.
  • A business owner from a neighboring town wants to explore opportunities to expand their business into Soweto.
  • A tourist visiting Soweto wants to discover popular attractions, local tours, and accommodations.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #cc5803 (Used for headings, navigation, and call-to-action buttons)
  • Secondary Color: #183a37 (Used for backgrounds, borders, and accents)

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Lato" (Used for main headings)
  • Body Font: "Open Sans" (Used for paragraphs and general content)
